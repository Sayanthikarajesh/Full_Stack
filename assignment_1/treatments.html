Treatment Option

Herbal Medicine

Herbal medicine uses plants and plant extracts to treat various health conditions. It is often customized based on the individual's constitution and health needs.

Panchakarma

Panchakarma is a detoxification process that involves five therapeutic treatments. It is designed to cleanse the body of toxins and restore balance.

Dietary Therapy

Dietary therapy focuses on personalized nutrition based on your dosha. It emphasizes whole foods and balancing the body's energies.

Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy utilizes essential oils from plants for therapeutic benefits. It can enhance emotional and physical well-being through scent.

Yoga and Meditation

Yoga and meditation promote physical and mental health through postures, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices.

Ayurvedic Massage

Ayurvedic massage incorporates herbal oils and specific techniques to promote relaxation and improve circulation.

Rejuvenation Therapy

This therapy focuses on revitalizing the body and mind, often involving treatments that promote longevity and vitality.

Akshitarpana

An ancient Ayurvedic remedy for ailments related to the eyes. This treatment is known to cure glaucoma, night blindness and other eye related complaints.

Consultation and Assessment

An initial consultation assesses your unique constitution and health needs, guiding the selection of appropriate treatments.
