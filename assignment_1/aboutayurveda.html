Principles of Ayurveda

Ayurveda emphasizes a holistic approach to health, focusing on the balance of body, mind, and spirit. It is based on the belief that health is determined by a balance between three energies, known as doshas: Vata, Pitta, and Kapha.

Dietary Recommendations

Ayurvedic diets are tailored to balance an individual's dosha, focusing on fresh, seasonal, and organic foods that align with one's body type.

Herbal Remedies

Ayurveda uses natural herbs and plants to promote healing, detoxification, and rejuvenation of the body, targeting specific doshas and health concerns.

Yoga & Meditation

Yoga and meditation are integral to Ayurveda. These practices help to strengthen the body, calm the mind, and create a deeper sense of self-awareness.

Detoxification

Ayurvedic detoxification techniques, such as Panchakarma, aim to cleanse the body of toxins, promote healing, and restore balance to the doshas.
